Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Third Peak Studio respects your privacy. We design our products to collect only the data you choose to share and only what is needed for app functionality. We never sell your data.

What we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as account details and any optional inputs you add to the app. We also collect limited technical data required to keep the app secure and working properly.

How we use data

We use your data to deliver app features, improve your experience, and support you when you ask for help. Data is used only at your discretion and for the purposes you expect.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising. We only share data when required to deliver the service (for example, trusted infrastructure providers), and only with appropriate safeguards.

Your choices

You can review, update, or delete your data at any time within the app settings. If you need help, contact us and we'll assist.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, email us at privacy@thirdpeakstudio.com.
