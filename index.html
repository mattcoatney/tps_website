Mission

To craft digital tools that help people live healthier, simpler, more intentional lives.

What We Build

Third Peak Studio is a small, independent studio creating focused data and AI applications. Our products are designed to support everyday decisions, habits, and well-being -- whether that's finding more time, moving more, sleeping better, or simply feeling less overwhelmed.

Our Approach: Direct-to-Human (DTH)

Most technology is designed for clicks, views, or conversions. Ours is designed for people. Direct-to-Human means:

  • Clear purpose and gentle interfaces
  • Respect for attention, privacy, and time
  • Features that support real-world behavior, not endless engagement
  • A focus on long-term benefit over short-term metrics

Technology should feel like support, not pressure.
